- repeats: 1
- network core plan: host_logical_cores=20, max_workers=16, cores_per_worker=1, mode=windows-powershell-processor-affinity

## Theory Baseline

The paper sets T=N/M and chooses B=M log(N/M). Under the stated BaseFold-like underlying PCS costs, Protocol 11 targets O(N/M) work per prover and O(M log^2(N/M)) verifier/proof size. This benchmark records N, M, measured T, and the B target for each row so measured data can be compared against that model.
